The vision of Tradelink

        TradeLink’s vision is to provide an efficient, secure, and intelligent cross-chain asset management platform through the integration of blockchain and artificial intelligence. The platform aims to simplify the trading process by optimizing transaction paths and fees in real time, helping users easily transfer assets across chains. With decentralized technology and AI-driven recommendations, TradeLink aspires to become a bridge connecting major blockchains and DeFi ecosystems, promoting the adoption of Web3 and decentralized finance, and offering global users a more convenient, intelligent, and secure digital asset management experience.

Development roadmap

Phase 1 (0-3 months): Launch of Core Features

• Develop and launch a cross-chain smart aggregator supporting major blockchains such as Ethereum and BNB Chain.

• Provide real-time asset management functionality for multi-chain wallets to simplify user operations.

Phase 2 (3-6 months): Feature Optimization and Expansion

• Upgrade the AI path optimization algorithm to enable dynamic gas fee prediction and transaction timing recommendations.

• Launch a decentralized cross-chain bridge feature to achieve more efficient cross-chain transfers.

Phase 3 (6-12 months): Tokenomics and Governance Module

• Launch liquidity mining and reward programs to encourage users to actively participate in building the platform ecosystem.

• Implement a community governance module, allowing users to vote on platform development proposals through $TLINK holdings.

Phase 4 (12-24 months): Ecosystem Expansion and Global Promotion

• Integrate more decentralized protocols (e.g., Aave, Uniswap) to support cross-chain DeFi operations.

• Expand into the GameFi and NFT sectors, offering multidimensional asset management and trading services.
